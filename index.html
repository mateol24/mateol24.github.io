Personal Finance

Personal finance is a very important subject which, unfortunately, is not taught in the high school. In today's world it is crucial to have some basic knowledge of this subject in order to manage your income and expenses and to create a safety net for your future. Below are the top five personal finance tips which would ultimately show positive results if applied with persistence.
Top five personal finance tips:

  1. Create a budget.
  2. Track your Net Worth.
  3. Write down all your DEBT.
  4. Live Below Your Means (LBYM).
  5. Take care of your credit score.
