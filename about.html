Who Am I?

I am Mateo Lamnije from Albania currently studying at LCC International University in Klaipeda, Lithuania. I am in my last year of studies in Intenrational Business Administration. As a student at a Liberal Arts university, I strongly believe that having knowledge from different fields can help anyone become a better investor and that is why I try to read and learn as much as I can about politics, technology, science, and of course finance.
