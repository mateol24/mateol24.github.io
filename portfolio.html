Investopedia Simulator

Investopedia Simulator is a popular game that simulates investing in the stock market. It provides you with some initial virtual money in your account which you can use to invest in different companies. The simulator follows the actual movements in the stock market and it shows all the neccessary information to track your investments.I have been playing this game for almost two years now and I love it. The main thing that I appreciate about this game is that it helped me see how would my investments perform if I would have invested real money in them. Because of the lack of funds to make real investments, I used this game to see if I am in the right direction to perform real investments in the near future.
